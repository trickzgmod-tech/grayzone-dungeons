About

GrayZone Dungeons is a fully modular dungeon experience created in Garry's Mod. Explore mysterious realms, defeat bosses, and collect tiered loot in an ever-expanding universe. Built with passion for depth, replayability, and adventure — it’s more than a server, it’s a world beneath GrayZone.

The Forgotten Depths

Beneath the ruins of GrayZone lies a labyrinth consumed by whispers and forgotten magic. The air is thick with decay, and light bends in unnatural ways. Legends tell of a guardian bound to the void — The Hollow Warden — who guards treasures that defy mortal understanding.

  • Shifting corridors that reshape as you explore
  • Echoing spirits whisper warnings and riddles
  • Boss: The Hollow Warden, master of the void
  • Loot: Cursed relics and dark energy crystals
  • Hazards: creeping void mist that drains vitality
